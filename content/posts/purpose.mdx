The Purpose is to learn,﻿ to refine the learning according to the understanding gained by or given to the learner, and to pass the learned knowledge on to the worthy for the recipient to use and transfer the learned information in a way that is proper according to their understanding and their heritage and the origin of the knowledge. ﻿The underlying purpose is for knowledge to be passed on with the least amount of modification as feasibly possible, understanding that language and methods of knowledge transfer change over time.
